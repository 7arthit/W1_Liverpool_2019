LIVERPOOL PLAYER SEASON 2021 - 2022

NO.62

Caoimhin Kelleher

Position : Goalkeeper

National : Ireland

Date of Birth : 23/11/1998 | 23 Years | Height 178 CM

Kelleher joined Liverpool's academy from Ringmahon Rangers in summer 2015. He featured regularly for Liverpool's side during their pre-season programme of 2018 and was part of the squad that travelled to the United States for Liverpool's summer tour. In August 2018, he signed a new contract with Liverpool. He was an unused substitute for the 2019 UEFA Champions League Final against Tottenham Hotspur. In winning the Champions League, he became the 12th Irish footballer to do so and the first for over a decade.
